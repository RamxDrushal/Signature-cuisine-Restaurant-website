Pub Facilitie

Having a bar facility in a restaurant can be a significant asset, offering various advantages. It not only enhances the overall dining experience by providing a vibrant and social atmosphere but also increases the restaurant's revenue through the sale of a diverse range of beverages. The presence of a bar can encourage customers to spend more time in the establishment, leading to increased sales and profitability.

Pool Table

Incorporating a pool table facility within a restaurant can offer several advantages. Beyond providing an additional form of entertainment, it can elevate the overall customer experience, encouraging patrons to spend more time at the establishment. This can lead to increased food and beverage sales, contributing to higher profitability.

Playing area

Having a dedicated playing area within a restaurant can be highly beneficial, particularly for families and young diners. Such an area not only provides a safe and engaging space for children to play but also allows parents to enjoy their meals more comfortably.
